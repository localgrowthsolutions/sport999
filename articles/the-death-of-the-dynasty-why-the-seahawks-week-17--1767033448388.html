THE TACTICAL "WAR ROOM": ANALYZING THE INVISIBLE GAME

Without their starting anchors, Seattle’s defensive shape is going to look like a disorganized low block with no exit strategy.

It’s a brave system, but it requires world-class discipline. With the current inactives, that discipline evaporates. We are going to see a massive xG underperformance today. The Panthers’ offensive coordinator will be licking his chops at the prospect of targeting the replacement linebackers in the seam.

Their inability to control the half-spaces will allow Carolina to transition from defense to attack with sickening ease. The verticality that Pete Carroll loves so much will be replaced by sideways passing and desperate clearances. It’s going to be a long afternoon for the 12s.
