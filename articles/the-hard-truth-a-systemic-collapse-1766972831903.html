THE HARD TRUTH: A SYSTEMIC COLLAPSE

It's tactical suicide from a leadership that has forgotten how to protect its most valuable assets.

They offer "verticality"—a quick rise to wealth—that the traditional football academies simply cannot match.

This is "Total Football" in its most perverted form. Everyone is involved, and there is no escape. The state’s inability to provide a "low block" of social security has left the youth to be picked off in the transition. It’s shambolic.
