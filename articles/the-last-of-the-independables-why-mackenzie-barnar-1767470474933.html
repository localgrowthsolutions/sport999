THE TACTICAL "WAR ROOM": ANALYZING THE BARNARD BLUEPRINT

Let’s talk about the "Invisible Game." Most fans watch the ball. We watch the spaces. Barnard’s genius was her mastery of the "half-spaces"—those awkward channels between the center-back and the wing-back where modern wingers love to play.
