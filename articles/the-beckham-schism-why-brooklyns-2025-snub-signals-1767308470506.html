Let’s talk high-press triggers. Or rather, the lack of them. When you build a team around Messi, Suarez, and Busquets, you are effectively "parking the bus" while you have the ball. The "Double Pivot" of Busquets and whatever youngster is tasked with doing his running is being bypassed with ease.
